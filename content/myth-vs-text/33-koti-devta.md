---
title: 33 Koti Devta
description: Clarifying the meaning of '33 Koti Devtas' in Vedic texts.
date: 2026-02-16
---

The phrase "33 Koti Devtas" does not mean 33 crore gods.

In Sanskrit, "Koti" can also mean category or type.

The Vedas refer to:

- 12 Adityas
- 11 Rudras
- 8 Vasus
- 2 Ashwini Kumaras
