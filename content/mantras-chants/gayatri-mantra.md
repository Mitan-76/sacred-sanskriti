The Gayatri Mantra is one of the most sacred Vedic mantras.

It is traditionally chanted for clarity of intellect and spiritual illumination.

Modern research suggests that rhythmic chanting influences:

- Breathing patterns
- Neural synchronization
- Emotional regulation
